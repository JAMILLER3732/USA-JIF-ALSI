Methodology

Price-Weighted Index (PWI) Formula:
ALSI = Σ(Constituent Stock Prices) ÷ Index Divisor × Base Scalar

Normalized to 100 on January 2, 2020. Divisor adjusts for splits, spin-offs, and constituent changes. Consistent with FGCU/RERI SWFL Stock Index methodology and the DJIA calculation method.

HAI Formula:
HAI = (Median Family Income ÷ Qualifying Income) × 100

Qualifying Income = Monthly P&I × 12 ÷ 0.28. Score of 100 = median household can exactly afford the median-priced home.

⊕ Alabama Context: Alabama’s lower median income (~$58K) is offset by significantly lower home prices, historically keeping the state more affordable than national averages despite elevated mortgage rates.
